Automated Emails

Here you can enabled automated email services.

If enabled, users will receive an email once weekly to notify them of new episodes of shows they have previously watched. Emails are sent every Sunday at noon. If a user has no new content, they will not receive emails. If there is a new episode of a show they have watched, and they have already watched the new episode, they will not receive an email about it.

If enabled, users will receive an email when they request content on Overseerr that is not yet released. Users may often request movies that are only released in cinemas, and are not available on streaming services. These automated emails will alert them to this fact.
